Brief History

If you Do Something You love. It's a lot easier and Takes on a lot more Purpose.

It Started six years ago. In a School Event. I was a member of the Student Council at That Time. During the event, my friend was a School Photographer. Out of Curiosity, I asked him if I could take pictures using his Camera. He Lends Me His Camera and Starts Taking Pictures. I was amazed and started liking it after the event. We talked about taking pictures, and he taught me how to shoot and other stuff. After that, I practised taking photos using my old Smartphone.

I Started To Save Money To buy a Camera by Taking a Part-Time Job as a fast food crew. I also saved some School Allowances. After a month, I purchased my First DSLR Camera. I practised again, but this time with a professional Camera. I invited my Friends as Models. As time went by, I Deepened my Knowledge of Photography.


I started To Establish my First Photography Firm, JR Photography. More and more opportunities Come to me and Give Exceptional Services, Specifically in School Events. after many years i Founded my First Studio "Street Studio Creatives". until now, we have provided precise and Exceptional Services in events and Photoshoots. Along with Professional Photographers and Videographers here in Bulacan.
